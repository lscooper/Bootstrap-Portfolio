About Me

Hi! My name is Laura Shearer Cooper. I like cats, funny mugs, and have strong opinions about user interface. I agree with the adage that user interface is like a joke: if you have to explain it, it isn't very good.


I love video games, and waste a ton of time on them. Right now, I'm on my seasonal Sims 4 kick, but I also play a lot of Bioware and Bethesda games, and an embarrassing amount of World of Warcraft.


I'm excited for this course because I love coding, and wanted to major in Computer Science in college. I went in, and loved it, but... [cue the sad violins] Computer Science takes a lot of calculus. A lot. I failed Calculus I not once.... not twice.... not thrice... But FOUR times. I'm determined to still work in the field I love, and I'm excited to work to make it a reality.
